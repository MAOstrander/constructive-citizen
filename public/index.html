Find your reps

Things you need to know about yourself to be a good citizen

  • Am I registered to vote?
  • If so, where? If not, what do I need to do and where do I need to go?
  • Who is my Mayor? (What city am I in?)
  • Who are my City Council members? What district am I in?
  • Who is my Governer
  • Who are my Congressional District members? What district am I in?
  • Who are my state Senators?

That's not all, it's best to understand how the pieces of the government fit together

  • What do each of those people listed above do?
  • How does the government work on a local level?
  • How does the government work on the state level?
  • How does the government work on the federal level?
  • What services does the government provide at various levels?

If you're fine with that, the final steps are to become active

  • Where do I vote?
  • When can I vote?
  • Who can I vote for?
  • What can I do to enact changes in policy?
